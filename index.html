Empowering Afghan Youth

FOROZ is dedicated to unlocking opportunities for Afghan youth through education, mentorship, and community support. Together, we're building a brighter future where every young person can pursue their dreams and reach their full potential.

Explore Our Programs

Our Mission

To empower Afghan youth by providing access to quality education, professional development opportunities, and a supportive community that fosters growth, innovation, and leadership.

Quality Education

Providing access to comprehensive educational resources, courses, and learning materials that prepare youth for future success in their chosen fields.

Mentorship Programs

Connecting young individuals with experienced mentors who guide, inspire, and support them on their journey to personal and professional excellence.

Community Building

Creating a vibrant, supportive community where Afghan youth can connect, collaborate, and grow together toward a shared vision of prosperity.
